# Contemporary India - Education Studies

Welcome to the comprehensive study material on Contemporary India and its Educational Systems. This documentation covers key aspects of education in modern India, including constitutional provisions, social diversity, educational policies, and equality in education.

This documentation is organized into five units:

### Unit 1: Constitutional & Educational Framework
Explore the constitutional provisions related to education, fundamental rights, professional education systems, right to education, secondary education structure, and social justice in education.

### Unit 2: Social Diversity
Understand the meaning, definition, and levels of social diversity in India, the interdisciplinary nature of education, and the role of education in creating a positive and inclusive environment.

### Unit 3: Educational Demands & Programs
Learn about the four pillars of education as proposed by the Delors Commission, and major educational initiatives like SSA (Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan), RMSA (Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan), and RUSA (Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan).

### Unit 4: Language Policy
Examine India's language policy as enshrined in the Constitution, its evolution during and after independence, and perspectives from great Indian thinkers on language in education.

### Unit 5: Equality in Education
Investigate types of social inequality, causes of inequality in education, and educational provisions for marginalized groups.
